Geometry Reference

Arc: Center (cx, cy), radius r. Semicircle from left (cx−r, cy) to right (cx+r, cy).

Track & fill share identical arc geometry. Only the endpoint differs.

Needle: From center, length ≈0.71 × r. Same angle as arc endpoint.

Angle formula: For headroom p (0.0–1.0):

θ = π × (1 − p)
arcEnd = (cx + r·cos(θ), cy − r·sin(θ))
needleEnd = (cx + needleLen·cos(θ), cy − needleLen·sin(θ))
large-arc-flag = (p ≥ 0.5) ? 1 : 0

Direction: 100% → needle right · 0% → needle left
