cc-hdrm — Design Directions

6 visual approaches for the menu bar popover and indicator. All use the headroom model, circular gauges, dense layout, and native macOS styling.

Direction 1: Classic Dual Gauge

Two large ring gauges side by side. Clean, symmetrical, iStat-inspired. The percentage lives inside the ring, countdown below. Maximum readability.
